ReflectaWord: A coding project

"A Toyota's a Toyota.", "Never odd or even.",and "A Santa at NASA." are all examples of Palindromes. A palindrome is a word, phrase or a sequence that reads the same backward as forward. This programme checks whether the text entered by the user is a PALINDROME

Instructions

Enter a phrase, word or a piece of text and click the button to check if it is a Palindrome
